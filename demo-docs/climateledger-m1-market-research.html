1. Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the voluntary carbon market (VCM), covering 12 active competitors, current pricing structures, and strategic gaps that ClimateLedger is positioned to address. The VCM is projected to reach $50B by 2030 (McKinsey, 2023). Our analysis identifies a critical gap in transparent, on-chain carbon credit issuance and retirement tracking.

2. Market Overview

The voluntary carbon market enables companies and individuals to offset emissions outside of regulated compliance schemes. Key drivers include ESG mandates, net-zero commitments, and increasing regulatory scrutiny of greenwashing. Market volume exceeded 500 MtCO₂e in 2024.

4. Pricing Analysis

Credit prices vary significantly by project type and verification standard:

  • Nature-based solutions (forestry/REDD+): $5–$50/tCO₂e
  • Renewable energy credits: $1–$8/tCO₂e
  • Direct air capture (DAC): $300–$1,000/tCO₂e
  • Blue carbon (ocean/mangrove): $20–$120/tCO₂e

Platform fees range from 0.2% (exchange models) to 15%+ (consulting-led models). SaaS pricing averages $12,000–$48,000 annually for enterprise clients.
