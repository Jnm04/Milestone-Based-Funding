Re: Letter of Intent — 3-Month Pilot Program for On-Chain Carbon Credit Tracking

Dear Dr. Hoffman,

Nexora Energy AG ("Nexora") is pleased to express its intent to participate as a pilot partner in ClimateLedger's on-chain carbon credit tracking and retirement platform.

Nexora Energy is a Swiss-based renewable energy company managing a portfolio of 14 wind and solar assets across Central Europe, with annual CO₂ offset obligations of approximately 45,000 tCO₂e under our corporate net-zero commitment by 2035.

Following our evaluation of ClimateLedger's technical architecture and competitive positioning, we believe the platform addresses key shortcomings in existing registry infrastructure, particularly regarding double-counting risk and audit trail transparency.

Scope of the Pilot Program (3 months, April–June 2026):

  • Tokenization and on-chain registration of 500 tCO₂e from Nexora's existing Verra-certified forestry credits
  • Integration testing with Nexora's internal ESG reporting system (SAP Sustainability Control Tower)
  • Real-time retirement tracking for quarterly regulatory disclosure
  • Joint evaluation of the platform's quality scoring methodology against Nexora's internal standards

Nexora agrees to provide timely access to relevant project documentation, assign a dedicated technical contact, and participate in a structured feedback session at the end of the pilot period.

This Letter of Intent is non-binding and does not constitute a commercial agreement. A formal Data Processing Agreement and Pilot Services Agreement will be negotiated and executed prior to program commencement.

We look forward to working with ClimateLedger and contributing to a more transparent voluntary carbon market.

Yours sincerely,
